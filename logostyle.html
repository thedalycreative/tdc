Images & Logo Guidelines

Logo Usage

  • Always use the original, high-resolution files. Do not stretch, distort, or recolour the logo.
  • Logos and images should be placed in ./tdcreative-site/images/ in your project.
  • Image file naming convention: ImageName-## (e.g., LOGO-01.png, HEADER-01.jpg, SWIRL-01.png).
  • For web: use <img src="./tdcreative-site/images/LOGO-01.png" alt="The Daly Creative logo">.
  • Logo background: Use the transparent version when overlaying on coloured backgrounds. For dark backgrounds, ensure the logo is clearly visible and maintains contrast.
  • Logo minimum size: 120px width on web, 32px for favicon/small icons.
  • Keep padding of at least 16px around the logo in any layout.
  • Do not overlay text, graphics, or patterns on top of the logo.

Image & Brand Visuals: Style Guide

  • Photography: Use bright, natural light, authentic Perth/Aussie environments, real people and places. Avoid generic or cliché stock images.
  • Brand Accent: Overlay the SWIRL-01 graphic subtly in corners or backgrounds for consistent branding.
  • Consistency: Maintain the logo’s gradient, shape, and padding. Never use drop shadows, outlines, or filters unless specified in campaign guidelines.
  • Alt-text: Always describe logos and brand images accurately for accessibility and SEO (e.g. alt="The Daly Creative logo").
  • Formats: Use PNG for logos/graphics, JPG for photographs. Use SVG only if provided in original assets.
